About

Indigenous Nigerian EPC company incorporated on the 9th June, 2014 with unique ability to seamlessly integrate engineering, procurement, and construction phases of any project providing a holistic approach that streamlines their execution and minimizes risks for project owners. Whether navigating complex engineering challenges, delivering turnkey solutions, or effectively managing risks, Dinox brings a comprehensive approach to deliver efficient project execution.

Dinox is currently diversifying and evolving in specialty and expertise in the oil and gas, logistics, renewable energy, power generation, power transmission, power distribution, civil engineering projects and many more.
