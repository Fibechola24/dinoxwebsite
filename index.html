Dinox Engineeering Ltd

Indigenous Nigerian EPC company incorporated on the 9th June, 2014 with unique ability to seamlessly integrate engineering, procurement, and construction phases of any project providing a holistic approach that streamlines their execution and minimizes risks for project owners.

Whether navigating complex engineering challenges, delivering turnkey solutions, or effectively managing risks, Dinox brings a comprehensive approach to deliver efficient project execution.

Dinox is currently diversifying and evolving in specialty and expertise in the oil and gas, logistics, renewable energy, power generation, power transmission, power distribution, civil engineering projects and many more.

Frequenlty Asked Questions

Dinox Engineering places a strong emphasis on adhering to all local and national regulations, codes, and standards in its engineering designs. Our team meticulously examines the requirements and ensures that the designs not only meet but exceed these standards where possible. By staying updated on regulatory changes and collaborating with regulatory bodies, we integrate the latest requirements into our designs to ensure full compliance

Dinox Engineering takes a comprehensive approach to procurement management, starting from supplier selection to transportation and delivery of materials to the construction site. We develop a detailed procurement management plan that outlines schedules for acquiring each item, continually adjusting it to maintain project timelines. Our focus on supply chain management involves monitoring inventory levels, lead times, and potential disruptions to ensure smooth operations. Through negotiations and strategic contracting, we secure competitive pricing and favorable terms to facilitate timely deliveries

Dinox Engineering prioritizes quality control and safety throughout the construction phase. We enforce strict quality assurance criteria and specifications, conducting thorough inspections and tests to validate the quality and operational integrity of completed scopes. Any identified shortcomings or challenges are promptly addressed to maintain quality standards. Additionally, we implement robust on-site safety protocols, adhering to regulatory guidelines and industry best practices to mitigate risks and ensure the safety of all personnel involved in the project.

Services

Turnkey Solution

we offer turnkey solution, meaning project owner receives a fully operational building or project at the end of the process. This eliminates the need for separate contracts for design, procurement, and construction, simplifying an owner's responsibilities. Dinox handles all aspects of the project, from inception to completion. And we offer non-turnkey project where we provide any of engineering, procurement or construction solution to project owners.
